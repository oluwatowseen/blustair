About Blustair

Blustair delivers the best technological solution for your company, combining top talent with a scientific approach to quality and efficiency.

Purpose

Blustair raises the bar in terms of quality of delivery.

Blustair Consulting brings forward years of knowledge and experience and utilizes them to provide its clients with value-added benefits. Since established in 2010 by a local group of professional and enthusiastic team, the goal was offering a unique and niche solutions with great added value. Today Blustair is one of fastest growing ICT solutions company with specialization in hardware & software solutions. Since inception, Blustair has continue to raise the bar in terms of quality of delivery and the capability of available resources. We ensure service delivery is within specified time, cost, and specification by adopting a professional but flexible approach to our engagements.

Strength

Blustair ensures technological tools required are available.

We are well equipped to critically analyze business needs, design and implement a best-fit technological solution and stabilize deployed solutions. We ensure technological tools required to facilitate the adoption of new systems are available and we provide support through the maturity phase of deployed solutions.

Motivation

Blustair is motivated by the need to deliver.

Blustair is motivated by the need to deliver value that surpasses the expectations of our clients. We harness our wealth of experience from the deployment of technology solutions across the energy, manufacturing, ecommerce, real estate, public and financial sectors. We leverage our industry knowledge of value chains and best practices for processes and systems in ensuring our clients are competitive in their respective sectors.

Promise

Blustair delivers much more than just technology.

We ensure the highest level of satisfaction with our clients through commitment to excellent service delivery, best industry expertise and a quality network of partners. We work with trusted partners that provide expertise, support, and insight that enables a successful working relationship with our clients. Our clients long term success is our motivation. Our team delivers much more than just technology, Blustair’s objective is to fully understand its clients’ operational processes and to deliver significant added value to its clients by developing solutions that dramatically optimize clients’ processes and enhance their ability to adapt to changes.
